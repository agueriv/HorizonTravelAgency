Travel in United Kingdom

Discover London

3 Days 2 Persons
Discover London's iconic cultural sites, from the imposing Buckingham Palace to the impressive collections of the British Museum. Immerse yourself in the city's rich history by exploring the majestic Tower of London and ancient fortifications, while on the South Bank experience modernity with the Tate Modern and the vibrant urban atmosphere. Culminate your day with views of the River Thames from charming restaurants, creating a complete experience of the British capital.

Day 1

In the morning you will visit Buckingham Palace to watch the changing of the guard. In the afternoon, you will visit Westminster Abbey and Big Ben. In the evening, you will dine in a traditional pub in Covent Garden, followed by a stroll through the illuminated Piccadilly Circus district.

Day 2

In the morning you will tour the British Museum to admire its vast collections, including the famous Rosetta Stone. In the afternoon you will stroll through the picturesque neighbourhood of Notting Hill, visiting Portobello Road Market and enjoying its bohemian charm. To round off the day, you will dine at an ethnic restaurant in Camden Town, followed by a tour of the area's night markets and lively music scene.

Day 3

You will start the day with a visit to the Tower of London to explore its ancient fortifications and the Crown Jewels. After lunch, you will take a walk through the trendy South Bank district, visiting the Tate Modern and strolling across the Millennium Bridge. To round off the day, you'll dine in a restaurant overlooking the River Thames, followed by a leisurely stroll through Hyde Park.

Travel to Bath

2 Days 4 Persons
Enjoy 2 days exploring the enchanting city of Bath, with its iconic Roman Baths, Georgian architecture and picturesque scenery. Immerse yourself in history at the baths complex, explore the elegant Georgian-era buildings and relax in the beautiful gardens. Savour local cuisine in cosy restaurants and enjoy a peaceful retreat in this historic gem of southwest England.

Day 1

In the morning a visit to the famous Roman Baths awaits to immerse yourself in the history of the city. After lunch, take a tour of the majestic Georgian buildings, including the Royal Crescent and the Circus. Finish the day with dinner at a local restaurant, followed by a leisurely stroll through the gardens of the Royal Victoria Park.

Day 2

To start the day, take a trip to the nearby Prior Park Landscape Garden to enjoy the panoramic views and picturesque scenery. At lunchtime, visit the Jane Austen Centre to learn more about the life and work of the famous author. In the evening you will dine in a charming restaurant in the city centre, followed by a leisurely stroll through the illuminated cobbled streets.

Travel in Spain

Visit Barcelona

2 Days +4 Persons
Discover fascinating Barcelona, where modernist architecture merges with medieval history and Mediterranean gastronomy captivates the senses. On this two-day trip, explore the iconic Sagrada Familia and the charming Gothic Quarter, enjoy authentic tapas in El Born and marvel at the views from Parc Güell. Immerse yourself in the bustling life of Las Ramblas and relax by the sea in Barceloneta.

Day 1

Start the morning with a visit to the Sagrada Familia to marvel at Gaudi's unique architecture. In the afternoon you'll enjoy a tour of the Gothic Quarter, exploring Barcelona's Cathedral and narrow medieval streets. For dinner, you'll have a tapas dinner in the lively El Born neighbourhood, followed by a walk along Paseo de Gracia to admire the city's night lights.

Day 2

You will start the day visiting Parc Güell to enjoy the panoramic views and Gaudí's creativity. After lunch, you'll stroll along Las Ramblas, visiting the Boqueria Market and the Palace of Catalan Music. You'll dine at a seaside restaurant in Barcelona, followed by a relaxing stroll along the promenade.

Enjoy in Malaga

4 Days 3 Persons
Discover enchanting Malaga on an exciting 4-day trip, where history meets culture, golden beaches and exquisite Mediterranean gastronomy. From the museums dedicated to Picasso to the breathtaking views from Gibralfaro Castle, this itinerary invites you to immerse yourself in the richness of this southern Spanish coastal city. Get ready for an unforgettable experience in Malaga!

Day 1

Starting the trip, in the morning you will visit the Picasso Museum to explore the famous Malaga-born painter. Later that afternoon, you'll take a tour of the Alcazaba and Gibralfaro Castle for panoramic views of the city. To end the day, you'll dine in a traditional restaurant in the old town, followed by a stroll through the lively pedestrian streets.

Day 2

The second day begins with an excursion to the nearby town of Nerja, where you will visit the famous Nerja Caves. In the afternoon, you'll have free time to relax on Malaga's beaches or explore the historic centre and its picturesque squares. To round off the day you'll dine at a beachfront chiringuito for fresh seafood and sunset views.

Day 3

The penultimate day begins with an excursion to the Montes de Málaga Natural Park to go hiking and enjoy nature. For lunch you will visit a local wine press to learn about the wine tradition of the region and taste typical wines. To end the day, you'll dine in a restaurant specialising in Andalusian cuisine, followed by a live flamenco show.

Day 4

On the last day, you will start by visiting the Central Market of Atarazanas to enjoy the local atmosphere and try some tapas. At lunchtime, you will walk around the Port of Malaga and the Paseo Marítimo, with stops to enjoy sea views and take pictures. To end the trip, you'll have dinner in a restaurant overlooking the harbour, toasting to the wonderful days spent in Malaga.

Travel in France

Love in Paris

3 Days 2 Persons
Discover the unparalleled beauty and unrivalled charm of Paris on an exciting 3-day journey, where every corner breathes romance, history and culture. From majestic monuments to quaint Parisian neighbourhoods, this itinerary will take you through an unforgettable experience in the City of Light. Prepare to immerse yourself in the art, cuisine and romance of Paris as you explore its cobblestone streets and revel in its iconic sights!

Day 1

You will start the first day by visiting the iconic Eifferl Tower for panoramic views of the city. After lunch, you will tour the Louvre Museum to admire masterpieces such as the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. To round off the day, you'll dine in an elegant restaurant overlooking the Champs Elysées, followed by a romantic stroll along the banks of the Seine.

Day 2

In the morning you will visit the majestic Notre-Dame Cathedral and stroll through the picturesque district of Le Marais. In the afternoon, discover the splendour of the Palace of Versailles with a half-day excursion from Paris. Finish the day with dinner in a traditional Parisian bistro, followed by a visit to the lively Montmartre district and the Sacré-Coeur Basilica.

Day 3

Start the last day exploring the charming Saint-Germain-des-Prés district and visit the Musée d'Orsay to see the impressive collection of Impressionist art. After lunch, stroll through the Luxembourg Gardens and enjoy a relaxing picnic. To round off your trip, you'll dine in an intimate Latin Quarter, followed by an evening cruise along the Seine to admire the illuminated monuments of Paris.

Quick visit Lyon

2 Days 2 Persons
Discover the enchanting city of Lyon on an exciting 2-day trip, where history is intertwined with world-renowned gastronomy and a charming urban atmosphere. With a rich cultural heritage and vibrant culinary scene, Lyon offers a unique experience combining art, history and exquisite flavours. Prepare to immerse yourself in the cobbled streets of the ancient Roman city, indulge in authentic bouchons cuisine and explore the city's magnificent museums and parks. Welcome to Lyon!

Day 1

Start your tour by visiting the ancient Roman city of Fourvière, including the Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière and the Roman Theatre. You will have lunch in a traditional bouchon lyonnais to taste the authentic local cuisine. The day will end with a stroll through the Vieux Lyon quarter, with its cobbled streets and traboules, followed by dinner in a typical Lyonnais restaurant.

Day 2

After breakfast, start your last day by visiting the Muse de Beaux-Arts de Lyon to admire its impressive collection of European art. In the afternoon, stroll through the Parc de la Tête d'Or, the city's green lung, where you can enjoy a leisurely picnic or take a boat ride on the lake. To end your trip, you will dine at a Michelin-starred restaurant for a unique and sophisticated gastronomic experience.
