Travel to Spain

Discover the magic of Spain, a country that captivates with its rich history, awe-inspiring monuments and vibrant culture. Immerse yourself in the majesty of the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, an architectural masterpiece that will take your breath away with its unique design. Don't miss the opportunity to experience the passion of flamenco in Seville, where music and dance are intertwined in a unique artistic expression. Also, immerse yourself in colourful festivities such as the Feria de Abril.

Why travel to Spain

  • Living history in every street.
  • Culture that enchants and surprises.
  • Gastronomy that awakens the senses.
  • Beaches bathed in sunshine.
  • Party and contagious joy.

Spain awaits you with open arms, offering you an unforgettable mix of history, architecture and vibrant traditions - embark on an adventure that will awaken all your senses!

Principal Monuments

SEGOVIA AQUEDUCT

The majestic Aqueduct of Segovia stands as a centuries-old witness to Roman engineering, defying time with its imposing presence. With more than 2,000 years of history, this stone colossus is an architectural masterpiece that snakes through the Spanish city, silently narrating the exploits of an ancient civilisation. Its graceful arches and magnificent structure not only carry water, but also those who gaze upon it, through a fascinating journey into the breathtaking past.

Segovia, Spain

BURGOS CATHEDRAL

Burgos Cathedral, a Gothic architectural jewel, rises like a symphony of stone that conquers the senses. Its spires point skywards like guardians of centuries-old secrets, while its stained glass windows dance with the light, telling stories carved into every stone. In the heart of Burgos, this heavenly wonder is not only a sacred temple, but an artistic testament that leaves its visitors captivated by the greatness of the history it houses.

Burgos, Spain

FIELDS, CASTLES, IMPOSING LEGACY

Travel to England

Step into the elegance and history of England, a destination that captivates with its timeless charm and iconic monuments. From the majesty of London's Buckingham Palace to the grandeur of the Tower of London, every step immerses you in centuries of royal heritage. But England is not only rich in monuments, but also in traditions that add a unique touch to your experience. Indulge your palate with the famous English tea and explore lively local markets. To travel to England is to immerse yourself in the perfect fusion of history, culture and modern charm - discover the essence of British elegance at every turn!

Why travel to England

  • Historic treasure around every corner.
  • English charms is magical landscapes.
  • Rich culture, vibrant tradition.
  • Theatres, teas and unforgettable experiences.

Principal Monuments

BUCKINGHAM PALACE

A silent witness to British royalty, Buckingham Palace stands imposingly in the heart of London, a symbol of elegance and power. Its golden gates conceal corridors that echoed with the history of monarchs and momentous events. With its majestic façade and motionless royal guards, this palace is not only the abode of royalty, but also a gateway to the grandeur of an empire that endures to this day.

London, United Kingdom

STONEHENGE

Nestled in the mystical plains of England, Stonehenge emerges as an ancient enigma that defies logic and captivates the imagination. Its imposing megaliths, erected in perfect celestial harmony, whisper secrets of a past lost in the mists of time. This circle of prehistoric stones is not only an archaeological monument, but a portal to the magical essence of antiquity, where mystery intertwines with the majesty of nature.

Salisbury, United Kingdom

VINEYARDS, ART, IRRESISTIBLE CROISSANTS

Travel to France

Embark on a captivating journey through the essence of France, a country that dazzles with its timeless elegance and rich history. From the majesty of the Eiffel Tower to the awe-inspiring beauty of Mont Saint-Michel, every corner is an architectural masterpiece that transports you back in time.

Discover exquisite French gastronomy, from irresistible croissants to sophisticated Parisian cuisine. Immerse yourself in the cultural life of Paris, where museums such as the Louvre house priceless art treasures. Explore the enchanting vineyards of the Burgundy region and be seduced by the elegance of the French Riviera.

With lively festivals, picturesque markets and warm hospitality, France invites you to live a unique experience - let the magic of this country enchant you at every turn!

Principal Monuments

TRIUMPHAL ARCH

The Arc de Triomphe in Paris stands as an imposing monument that pays homage to France's historical grandeur. With its majestic arches and detailed sculptures, this Parisian icon is not only an architectural testament, but a guiding beacon through the tumultuous chapters of French history. From its summit, panoramic views of the City of Light bring to life the grandeur emanating from its stones, making it a timeless symbol of elegance and endurance.

Paris, France

EIFFEL TOWER

The Eiffel Tower, elegant iron lady gracefully rising above the Parisian skyline, is more than an icon; it is a testament to human daring and timeless beauty. Its intricate metal structures form a visual symphony that captures the imagination, while its panoramic views reveal the enchanting secrets of Paris. In every flash of light on its lattices, the Eiffel Tower celebrates the union between extraordinary engineering and the poetry of the Parisian skyline, seducing all with its timeless charm.

Paris, France
