About Horizon

Horizon is an international travel agency that is passionate about discovering new horizons. Our team of experts is dedicated to helping you plan the perfect trip, whether you're looking for a relaxing beach vacation or an adventurous expedition. We offer a wide range of travel services, including flights, hotels, rental cars, and tours. We also offer a variety of travel insurance options to protect you in case of any unexpected events.

We understand that planning a trip can be stressful, so we make it our goal to take care of everything for you. We'll work with you to create a personalized itinerary that fits your interests and budget. We'll also book all of your travel arrangements, so you can relax and enjoy your trip.

If you're looking for a travel agency that can help you discover new horizons, Horizon is the perfect choice. We'll make sure your trip is everything you've ever dreamed of.

Get to know our roots

Our History

Horizon Travel Agency is based in Malaga and has more than 5 years of experience in the sector. It was founded in 2015 by a group of professionals with extensive experience in the world of tourism. Horizon specialises in organising tailor-made trips for both individuals and companies.

Horizon has established itself as one of the leading travel agencies in Malaga thanks to its commitment to quality and customer service. The agency has won several awards, including the Tourist Excellence Award from the Junta de Andalucía in 2019.

Today, Horizon is one of the most important travel agencies in Spain. The agency has a team of more than 50 professionals and offers a wide range of services to its clients. Horizon is committed to quality and customer service, and has won several awards for excellence.
